## 7. Finding an Accountant

### What to Look For

- **CCPC experience** ‚Äî understands small business deduction, passive income rules
- **SR&ED specialist or partnership** ‚Äî either they do it in-house or have a dedicated SR&ED partner
- **Startup-friendly** ‚Äî won't charge $500/hr for basic bookkeeping questions
- **Cloud accounting** ‚Äî uses Xero, QuickBooks Online, or Wave
- **Proactive tax planning** ‚Äî not just filing, but structuring (salary vs dividends, HST optimization)

### SR&ED Specialist vs General Accountant

- **General accountant:** Does your T2 return, bookkeeping, HST. Cost: $1,500-$4,000/yr for a startup
- **SR&ED specialist:** Prepares the technical narrative + financial claim. Often works on **contingency** (25-30% of the refund, no upfront cost)
- **Best approach:** Get a general accountant for books/tax + a separate SR&ED firm on contingency
